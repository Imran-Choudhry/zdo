About Zindagi Development Organization

Registered non-profit working in Dadu District, Sindh, Pakistan — focused on social justice, flood relief, and sustainable development.

Our Vision: The organization envisions a balanced society, based on social justice and equality, is free from all types of discriminations and a poverty eradicated society where every one has space and opportunities for their own development.

Our Core Areas

Holistic development programs for Dadu's rural communities.

Flood Relief & Recovery

Emergency food, clean water, and shelter for flood-affected families in UC Sial and other submerged villages.

Quality Education

Non-formal schools and scholarships for out-of-school children in remote areas like Johi, Khairpur Nathan Shah.

Health & Hygiene

Mobile health camps, maternal health awareness, and WASH initiatives to prevent waterborne diseases.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training (sewing, handicrafts) and legal awareness for economic independence.

Clean Water Access

Installation of hand pumps and solar water filtration plants in drought-affected union councils.

Climate Resilience

Mangrove plantation, flood preparedness training, and sustainable farming workshops.

Our Impact: Real Stories from the Field

Latest update — September 6, 2025: Reaching flood victims in UC Sial.

Emergency Flood Response – UC Sial

When floodwaters swallowed homes in Sono Khan Jatoi, Mubarak Jatoi, and Balocho Khan Jatoi, ZDO with GCDT reached them by boats. We distributed cooked meals, boiled eggs, and clean water.

"We had lost everything, but the food and water gave us hope that we are not forgotten." — Flood-affected villager

Food & Nutrition Support

Provided hot cooked meals and protein-rich boiled eggs to hungry children and families stranded after the floods — a message of humanity and solidarity.

Safe Drinking Water

Delivered clean water to prevent waterborne diseases in flood-affected villages, ensuring health protection during the crisis.
